Geoff Scott is CTO at [OneEleven Financial Wellness](https://oneeleven.co){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}. He also runs [Growth Science](https://growthscience.co){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, an executive coaching practice, co-organizes [Table](https://tabledialogue.com){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, a community for entrepreneurs, and sits on the boards of [Saranam Center for Healing and Transformation](https://saranam.org){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} and [SIM New York Metro](https://nysim.org){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}.

He has built three startups and launched dozens of products for other companies. He has held leadership roles at three Inc. 500s and two Fortune 500s. Four of the companies he has worked with have exited, one through an IPO. He co-invented five issued US patents: four covering an AI digital therapeutic platform and one for a hardware device for medication adherence. He founded Hackerati, advising Viacom, The Huffington Post, Narrativ, and others, and held executive roles at DoubleClick, CBS, and The Princeton Review.

He created [Kindness Flywheel](https://kindnessflywheel.org){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, where he writes about leadership, technology, and what makes both work, and maintains several [open source AI projects](https://github.com/geoffscott){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}. He lives in the Northern Catskills. Find him on [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/geoffscott){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}.
